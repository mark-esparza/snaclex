Research use only

SnaCleX is provided for research and educational purposes only. Its outputs — interaction profiles, pocket detection, conservation scores, docking poses, and screening rankings — are approximate geometric and empirical heuristics computed from single static structures. Docking scores are relative, not calibrated affinities (not kcal/mol).

SnaCleX is not a medical device and is not for clinical, diagnostic, or treatment use. Do not rely on it for any decision affecting human or animal health. Always validate results with orthogonal evidence.
